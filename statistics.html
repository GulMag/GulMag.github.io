Probability Axioms

The concept of probability can be formally represented through a set of axioms. The Kolmogorov axioms are the foundations of probability theory introduced by Andrey Kolmogorov in 1933.

  1. The probability of an event is a non-negative real number.
  2. The probability that at least one of the elementary events in the entire sample space will occur is 1.
